# Health

Personal health records and tracking logs. **Not** JDS document-numbered (these are personal, not engineering deliverables), but organised with the same JDS principles — clear structure, consistent tables, and honest labelling.

| File | What it is |
|------|------------|
| [`blood-pressure-log.md`](blood-pressure-log.md) | Track BP readings over time, with measurement guidance and reference ranges |
| [`food-diary.md`](food-diary.md) | Daily food, drink, and symptom log for spotting patterns |

> **Important:** These are personal tracking tools, not medical advice. Share them with a healthcare professional rather than self-diagnosing. In an emergency, seek urgent care.

## Tips

- Fill them in **as you go**, not from memory at the end of the day.
- A trend over **two weeks or more** is what makes these useful to a clinician — bring them to appointments.
- Keep them private — health data is personal; nothing here is meant to leave your own records.
