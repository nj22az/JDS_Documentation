> **Important:** This is a personal tracking tool, not medical or dietary advice. If you are tracking symptoms (e.g. reflux, intolerance, blood sugar), share this diary with a healthcare professional rather than self-diagnosing.

## How to Use

- **Write it down as you go** — recording from memory at the end of the day misses things.
- **Be specific** — "2 slices wholemeal toast + butter" is more useful than "breakfast".
- **Note the context** — where you ate, with whom, how hungry you were (1–5), and your mood.
- **Track symptoms** — note anything you feel (bloating, energy dip, headache, reflux) and **how long after eating** it appeared. Patterns only show up over a couple of weeks.
- **Don't forget drinks and snacks** — water, coffee, alcohol, and nibbles all count.
