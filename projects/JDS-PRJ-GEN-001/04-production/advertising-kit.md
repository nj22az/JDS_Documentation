# Advertising Kit — The Garage Reset / Städa i Garaget

Ready-to-use copy for ads, social posts, and the Amazon A+ section. Two markets:
**EN (amazon.com / international)** and **SV (amazon.se, primary)**. The hero image for every
ad is the original **before → after** illustration (`03-assets/images/before-after*.png`) — it
shows the promise in one glance and is 100 % licence-clean (original art, safe to advertise).

---

## The problem, in one line (the ad's job is to make the reader feel this)

**EN:** Your garage became the house's dumping ground — the car's stuck outside, you can't find a
tool, and the cleanup you did in spring was gone by summer.

**SV:** Garaget har blivit husets soptipp — bilen står ute, du hittar inga verktyg, och
vårstädningen var borta till sommaren.

## Taglines (pick one per campaign)

**EN**
- Take back your garage — one weekend at a time.
- Stop cleaning your garage. Start building a system.
- From dumping ground to a garage that works *for* you.
- The order method real workshops run on — brought home.

**SV**
- Ta tillbaka ditt garage — en helg i taget.
- Sluta städa garaget. Börja bygga ett system.
- Från soptipp till ett garage som jobbar *för* dig.
- Proffsens ordningsmetod — hem till ditt garage.

## One-line hook (for an ad headline)

- **EN:** A 10-step program to take back your garage — one weekend at a time.
- **SV:** Ett 10-stegsprogram för att ta tillbaka ditt garage — en helg i taget.

## Short blurb (≤ 300 characters — social / Amazon strapline)

**EN:** The garage is the one room with no system, so it becomes the dumping ground. *The Garage
Reset* gives you a 10-step program built on the professional 5S method: declutter it once, then
keep it with ten minutes a week.

**SV:** Garaget är det enda rummet utan system — därför blir det soptippen. *Städa i Garaget* ger
dig ett 10-stegsprogram byggt på proffsens 5S-metod: rensa en gång, håll sedan ordningen med tio
minuter i veckan.

## Audience targeting (ad interests)

Homeowners and house/villa owners; DIY, woodworking, home improvement; decluttering / organizing
(KonMari, döstädning); car & motorcycle enthusiasts; garage-gym owners; **SV also:** villaägare,
radhus, fritidshusägare, "gör det själv".
