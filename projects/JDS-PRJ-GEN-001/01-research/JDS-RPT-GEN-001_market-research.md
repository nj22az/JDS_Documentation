## 1. Purpose

To establish whether a Swedish-language book about **decluttering and keeping order in
the garage, workshop and storage room** has a viable market: is the category proven, is
the specific niche already served, and is there genuine demand?

## 2. Summary of Findings

| Question | Finding |
|----------|---------|
| Is the decluttering category proven in Sweden? | **Yes** — multiple durable bestsellers |
| Does a dedicated garage/workshop book already exist (Swedish)? | **No** — only retailer guides and blog posts |
| Is there demand for the niche? | **Yes** — strong DIY/villa culture, heavy retailer investment |
| Does the author have a credible edge? | **Yes** — real engineer, 5S/workshop discipline |
| **Overall verdict** | **Defensible niche in a proven category — proceed** |

## 3. The Category Is Proven

Decluttering and home-organizing is one of the most reliable non-fiction categories in
the Swedish market, with repeat buyers and long shelf lives.

| Author | Book | Note |
|--------|------|------|
| Marie Kondo | *Konsten att städa* | 14M+ copies worldwide, 44 languages; large KonMari following in Sweden |
| Margareta Magnusson | *Döstädning* | Swedish-born global category (2017, Albert Bonniers); became a US TV show |
| Paulina Draganja ("Förvaringsdrottningen") | *Förvara smart*; *Organisera och förvara hemma* | Two bestsellers; translated to Polish, German, Portuguese |

The "ett projekt i veckan" (one project per week) structure used by Draganja confirms
that Swedish readers respond to **structured, actionable, do-it-this-week** formats —
directly relevant to how this book should be written.

## 4. The Niche Is Empty

Every proven title above targets the **home interior** — wardrobes, kitchens, room by
room. A search across the major Swedish channels for a book specifically about the
**garage / verkstad / förråd** returned **no dedicated book**:

- **Bokus / Adlibris / Akademibokhandeln** — organizing books are all home-focused
- **Amazon.se** — garage results are *storage products*, not books (or English-only e-books)
- The only Swedish garage-organizing content is **retailer guides and blogs**:
  Clas Ohlson, Bauhaus/Elfa, Byggahus.se, Garageinredning.se, GDS.se

This is the gap: a high-intent subject covered only by fragmented commercial content,
with no authoritative, structured book to own the shelf.

## 5. Demand Signals

```
  Proven category        Empty niche            Real audience
  ───────────────        ───────────            ─────────────
  Kondo · Magnusson      No Swedish             610k+ holiday homes
  Draganja               garage/workshop        deep villa & DIY culture
  (home-focused)         book exists            heavy retailer content spend
        │                     │                        │
        └─────────────────────┴────────────────────────┘
                              ▼
              Defensible niche in a proven market
```

- **Housing stock:** 610,000+ fritidshus plus a large villa base — garages, förråd and
  hobby workshops are near-universal among the target reader.
- **Retailer investment:** Clas Ohlson, Bauhaus (Elfa), Byggahus and dedicated firms
  (Garageinredning, Förvara.se) publish extensive garage-organizing guides — they spend
  because the audience converts.
- **Adjacent proof:** the home-organizing trend (Kondo, KonMari Sverige groups) shows a
  population already primed to buy structured decluttering methods.

## 6. Positioning & Differentiation

The author's advantage is the differentiator. Lifestyle authors avoid the garage because
it is technical — tools, fasteners, oils, fuels, seasonal and heavy equipment, safety.
A working marine/field-service engineer can credibly apply the **5S method** (the same
discipline JDS itself runs on) to that space.

**Proposed positioning:** the warm, project-by-project Swedish decluttering format —
applied with real workshop authority to the one room the bestsellers skip.

## 7. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|------------|
| Niche too narrow for a publisher | Med | Med | Self-publish option; pitch as series (garage → förråd → fritidshus) |
| Reader expects glossy product catalogue | Med | Low | Method-first, brand-neutral; photos of real spaces |
| Retailer free content seen as "good enough" | Med | Med | Book offers a complete *system*, not scattered tips |
| Author time | High | Med | Project-per-chapter structure; reuse 5S material |

## 8. Recommendation

**Proceed.** Develop the concept and outline (JDS-MAN-GEN-001) and draft the manuscript.
The category is proven, the specific niche is unoccupied in Swedish, demand indicators are
strong, and the author holds a credibility advantage that is hard to copy.
