# About the Author

**Nils Johansson** is a marine and field-service engineer who has spent most of his working life in
workshops, engine rooms, and on job sites where order is a matter of safety, not appearance. There he
learned the proven method professionals use to keep even the busiest and most demanding spaces running —
and eventually realised the same method works just as well in an ordinary home garage.

*The Garage Reset* is the result: the professional ordering method, written in plain language and adapted
for the home garage, workshop, and shed. It's a book by someone who has actually stood on the workshop
floor — not to make the garage look good in a photo, but to make it work when you need it.
