# A Last Word

Thank you for reading *The Garage Reset*. If the book helped you take back your garage, I'd ask you one
thing: **please leave a review where you bought it.** A few lines from you help the next person standing in
front of their own pile, wondering where to start.

And remember — the method doesn't stay in the garage. The five steps work in the storage locker, the cabin
shed, every space that's grown unruly. You haven't just put one room in order. You've learned a way of
thinking.

Good luck. Open the door.
