# Checklist: Your 10-Step Program

Tear out this page, or photograph it, and hang it in the garage. Tick off one step at a time.
There's no rush — one or two steps a weekend is plenty.

**Prepare**

- [ ] **Step 1 — Set the goal.** Take a "before" photo. Decide why. Pick a first weekend. *(ch. 1–2)*

**Sort**

- [ ] **Step 2 — Empty it out and look.** Carry everything out. Group by kind. *(ch. 3)*
- [ ] **Step 3 — Keep, lose, or relocate.** Three piles, one item at a time. *(ch. 4)*
- [ ] **Step 4 — Handle the dangerous stuff.** Fuel, paint, chemicals to the drop-off. *(ch. 5)*

**Set in Order**

- [ ] **Step 5 — Draw the zone map.** Five zones, by how you move. *(ch. 6)*
- [ ] **Step 6 — Get it off the floor.** Walls, pegboard, shelves, ceiling. *(ch. 7)*
- [ ] **Step 7 — Label and make it findable.** Find anything in three seconds. *(ch. 8)*

**Shine**

- [ ] **Step 8 — Shine it.** Sweep the floor, wipe the bench. Set the baseline. *(ch. 1–2)*

**Standardize**

- [ ] **Step 9 — Set the rules.** Back right away · one in, one out · empty packaging out · place first. *(ch. 9)*

**Sustain**

- [ ] **Step 10 — Sustain it.** Ten minutes a week. Spring and fall. *(ch. 10–11)*

---

**When every box is ticked you don't just have a clean garage — you have a system that keeps it
that way.**
