> **AFS 2017:3, 4 Kap. §17:** Employers with Class A or B equipment must have documented supervision routines, evaluated at least once per year, with a named physical person responsible.
>
> **Non-compliance penalty:** 10,000 — 100,000 SEK.

---

## 1. The 6 Mandatory Checks (AFS 2017:3, 2 Kap. §6)

Every supervision round must address these six points:

| # | Mandatory Check | Interval |
|:-:|----------------|:--------:|
| 1 | Equipment functions satisfactorily | \_\_\_\_ months |
| 2 | No leaks have occurred | \_\_\_\_ months |
| 3 | No harmful external or internal impact | \_\_\_\_ months |
| 4 | No other faults or deviations | \_\_\_\_ months |
| 5 | Equipment correctly marked (labels, warnings) | \_\_\_\_ months |
| 6 | Prescribed inspections have been carried out | \_\_\_\_ months |

## 6. Findings Management

| Severity | Definition | Action | Timeline |
|----------|-----------|--------|:--------:|
| **Critical** | Immediate safety risk | Out of service, notify operator | Immediate |
| **Major** | Will deteriorate to critical | Plan repair, close monitoring | 30 days |
| **Minor** | Noted, no immediate risk | Monitor, plan maintenance | 90 days |
| **Observation** | Worth monitoring | Note, observe trend | Next round |
