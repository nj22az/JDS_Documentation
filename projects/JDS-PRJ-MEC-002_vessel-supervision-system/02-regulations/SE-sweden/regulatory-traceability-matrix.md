## 1. Purpose

This matrix maps every element of the Vessel Supervision System (JDS-PRJ-MEC-002) to the specific sections of AFS 2017:3 that it satisfies. It is the compliance evidence trail.

The supervision program manual (JDS-MAN-MEC-002) is the authoritative working document. This matrix proves it meets regulatory requirements.

---

## 2. How to Use This Document

- **During audits:** Present this matrix to demonstrate regulatory coverage
- **When updating the system:** Check that no regulatory requirement is left uncovered
- **When adapting to other countries:** Create a new matrix for the target regulation

## 10. Amendment-Specific Changes

### AFS 2019:1 Impact on Supervision Programs

| Change | Affected Section | Impact |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| Piping explicitly in scope | Kap. 2, §3 | Supervision programs must include piping systems above threshold |
| Interval extension provisions refined | Kap. 4, §15-16 | Operators may apply for extended intervals with documented justification |

### AFS 2020:10 Impact on Supervision Programs

| Change | Affected Section | Impact |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| Digital documentation accepted | Kap. 2, §5 | Electronic records (including Git-managed) satisfy documentation requirements |
| Extended deadlines during extraordinary circumstances | Kap. 4, §15-16 | Temporary provision: inspection deadlines may be extended with risk assessment |
| Remote verification permitted | Kap. 4, §14 | Where physical access is restricted, remote methods may supplement inspection |

### AFS 2022:2 Impact on Supervision Programs

| Change | Affected Section | Impact |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| Revised competence requirements | Kap. 2, §4 | More specific requirements for supervision personnel competence documentation |
| Updated classification thresholds | Kap. 3, Bilaga 2 | Certain equipment categories reclassified — review all vessel classifications |
| Modification provisions clarified | Kap. 6 | Clearer requirements for when modifications affect classification |
