# Book One — The Venture (outline)

**Era:** 1603–1626. **Spine:** Maggie. **Target:** 75–85k words in three parts.
**Standalone promise:** a reader who has never seen the anthology gets a complete novel — a widow who keeps a room on a hungry river, the boy she couldn't stop, the woman who refused to be property, and the man who came back wrong — and closes the book knowing exactly what the Company is, one generation before anyone in it does.

**Canon sources:** anthology ch1 (The Boy Who Signed), ch2 (Dutch Courage), ch3 (The Man Who Came Back Wrong). Their facts bind; their scenes expand.

## POV plan

Close third, rotating by chapter, Maggie primary (she anchors ≥8 chapters and opens and closes the book). Tom, Mara, Hendricks, Daniel, and Nell each get chapters. The narrator is the anthology's narrator: the room, dry, ledger-minded.

## Repertory casting, Book One

Keeper: Maggie · Warner: Hendricks · Company Man: Pryce (with the muster clerk as his junior echo in ch4) · Bruiser: Rook · Refuses-to-be-property: Mara · Haunted Returner: Daniel · Appetite: Carter.
