# Part II — The Years Between

*1604–1625*

The fleet is gone and the district goes on being the district: ships out, ships in, the count under the counter growing. These are the years the histories skip — a keeper, a barmaid, a waterman and a young chandler holding one room steady while, very far away and completely unheard, the Company turns into the thing it was always going to be.
