# Part III — The Man Who Came Back Wrong

*1626*

The pamphlets have had the massacre for two years. Now the house gets the man. What the Company's paper did at Amboyna, and what its paper does after, come up the same stairs in the same season — and a keeper, a barmaid, a chandler and a Dutchman find out what twenty-three years of keeping a room was for.
