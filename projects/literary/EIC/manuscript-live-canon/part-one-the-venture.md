# Part One: The Venture

*1603–1657*

Tom enters the Prospect at twenty-two with a seaman's hands, an inherited debt and a Company's paper. Across six movements he returns from successive voyages while he and Maggie grow into the book's first true relationship: pride disguised as scolding, disappointment that cannot be escaped, reconciliation without a speech, and care passed onward after Maggie is gone.
